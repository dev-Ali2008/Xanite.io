Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Xanite Emulator

What is Xanite?

Xanite is a fast and optimized emulator for Android that supports OG Xbox and Xbox 360 games. It's built to bring classic Xbox titles to mobile devices with smooth performance and high compatibility.

What platforms does Xanite support?

Xanite is only available for Android. Other platforms like iOS or PC are not supported.

What consoles does it emulate?

Xanite emulates:
- Original Xbox (2001)
- Xbox 360 (2005)

Is Xanite free to use?

Yes! Xanite will offer two versions: a free version and a premium version called Xanite Gold!.

Where can I download it?

You can download the latest version from the Downloads section on our main page.

Does Xanite come with games?

No. Xanite does not come with any games. You must provide your own legally-owned game files.

Is Xanite safe? I heard it has a virus.

That's false. Xanite is clean and safe to use.
- No viruses, malware, or spyware.
We encourage transparency — check out our code/builds via GitHub.

Where can I report bugs or get help?

You can join our Discord or post issues/ideas on GitHub to get support or suggest features.
