Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Xanite Emulator.

What is Xanite Emulator?

Xanite is an open-source Xbox Original emulator for Android devices.

Is Xanite free to use?

Yes, Xanite is completely free and open-source.

What are the minimum requirements?

Android 8.0+, 4GB RAM (8GB recommended), Snapdragon 845 or equivalent.

How can I improve performance?

Close background apps, lower resolution settings, and keep your device cool.

Does it support controllers?

Yes, full support for Bluetooth and USB controllers.

When will it be released?

Xanite is currently under development. Join our Discord for updates!
