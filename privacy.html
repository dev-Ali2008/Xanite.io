Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how Xanite Emulator protects your data and respects your privacy rights.

Last Updated: December 2025

Data Collection

What we collect and what we don't

Xanite Emulator is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe in minimal data collection and maximum transparency.

What We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, or physical address
  • Device Identifiers: IMEI, MAC address, or unique device IDs
  • Game Files: Your game ROMs, ISOs, or save files
  • Usage Data: What games you play, for how long, or when
  • Location Data: GPS location or IP-based location tracking
  • Contact Information: Contacts from your device or social media

What We MAY Collect (Optional)

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to fix bugs (opt-in)
  • Performance Metrics: Aggregated, anonymous performance data (opt-in)
  • App Usage Statistics: Basic usage patterns to improve the app (opt-in)
Data Type Collected Purpose Storage
Personal Information ❌ Never - -
Game Files ❌ Never - -
Crash Reports ⚠️ Optional Bug fixing 30 days
Performance Data ⚠️ Optional Optimization 90 days

Data Usage & Sharing

How we use data and who has access

Any data collected (all optional) is used exclusively to improve Xanite Emulator and provide better user experience.

Crash Reporting

Anonymous crash data helps us identify and fix bugs faster. No personal information is included in crash reports.

Performance Optimization

Aggregated performance data helps us optimize Xanite for different device types and Android versions.

Usage Analytics

Anonymous usage patterns help us understand which features are most popular and need improvement.

Data Sharing Policy

We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade any user data. We DO NOT share data with third-party advertisers or analytics companies. Any collected data is used internally only for improving Xanite Emulator.

Legal Disclosures

  • We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process
  • We may share aggregated, anonymous data for research or statistical purposes
  • In the event of a merger or acquisition, user data may be transferred to the new entity

Your Rights & Controls

You're in control of your data

You have complete control over your data. Here are your rights and how to exercise them:

Right to Access

You can request information about what data we have about you (if any).

Right to Delete

You can request deletion of any data we have collected about you.

Right to Opt-Out

You can disable all data collection in the app settings at any time.

How to Control Your Data

Within Xanite Emulator:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy to control data collection options
  • Toggle "Crash Reporting" on/off as desired
  • Toggle "Performance Analytics" on/off as desired
  • Toggle "Usage Statistics" on/off as desired

Via Email: Contact us at privacy@xanite-emulator.com for data requests.

Data Retention Period

  • Crash reports: Retained for 30 days then automatically deleted
  • Performance data: Retained for 90 days then automatically deleted
  • Usage statistics: Retained for 90 days then automatically deleted
  • All data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users

Security Measures

How we protect your data

We take security seriously and implement multiple layers of protection for your data.

Open Source Transparency

Xanite is open source. Anyone can review our code on GitHub to verify our privacy claims.

Data Encryption

All transmitted data is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols.

Secure Storage

Data is stored on secure servers with limited access and regular security audits.

Local Data Protection

On Your Device:

  • Game files remain 100% local on your device
  • Save files are stored locally in app-private directories
  • No data leaves your device without your explicit permission
  • All local storage uses Android's built-in security features

Your Responsibility

While we protect your data on our end, you also play a role in security:

  • Keep your Android device updated with the latest security patches
  • Use strong passwords or biometric locks on your device
  • Only download Xanite from official sources (GitHub or our website)
  • Be cautious of third-party mods or modified versions
