Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Xanite Emulator does not collect any personal data from your device. We do not track, store, or share your personal information, game data, or device identifiers.

Game Files

All game files remain locally on your device. We never upload your game ROMs or ISOs to any servers.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies for basic functionality. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used.

Security

Your data is protected by local storage encryption. All app permissions are clearly disclosed during installation.

Open Source

Xanite is open-source software. You can inspect the code on our GitHub repository.

Changes
